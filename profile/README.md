## What we publish

**Weekly.** One sharp thesis per issue. Direct prose, compressed logic, operators introduced as proof. Published every Wednesday.

**Monthly.** A long-form synthesis pulling threads from the month's Weeklies into one continuous essay. Published the first of the month. Drafted by an autonomous pipeline that learned the publication's voice from the back catalog, then approved by a human before publishing.

**The Agent Index.** A curated index of tools an AI agent can actually use, with a strict programmatic-path test for inclusion. Most "AI tools" lists are dumps. This one excludes more than it includes.

**Tutorials.** Agent-building patterns where an AI agent measurably beats a human at a specific task. Each entry contains a before-state, an after-state, and one sentence explaining the advantage. Generated by an autonomous engine, gated by an eight-check verifier, improvable through community contributions.

Both projects accept community contributions through pull requests. The same editorial bar applies whether the contribution comes from the autonomous engine or from a human contributor. Read each repo's `CONTRIBUTING.md` for the specifics.

## Editorial position

The future involves builders creating businesses *by* AI agents and *for* AI agents. Not "AI as a feature" — AI as the customer, the operator, and the substrate. The Builder Weekly is the publication for the people building that future.

We don't cover every AI announcement. We cover the patterns that survive a year of production use.
